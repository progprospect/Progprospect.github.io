Careers

Build software with people who care about craft, clarity, and real outcomes.

ProgProspect is growing a team of engineers, designers, strategists, and operators who want to build intelligent software systems for modern businesses without losing sight of quality and user experience.

Benefits

A work environment designed for serious builders.

Meaningful technical problems

Work on SaaS platforms, AI applications, enterprise systems, APIs, automation, and cloud architecture instead of shallow one-off pages.

High standards, calm execution

We value careful planning, clear communication, maintainable code, and thoughtful delivery over chaos disguised as speed.

Growth through ownership

Team members are encouraged to understand the business problem, propose better paths, and own the quality of their work.

Remote-friendly collaboration

We design work around documentation, async clarity, focused calls, and transparent progress so people can do deep work.

Culture

We are building a company where taste and engineering discipline reinforce each other.

Think before building

We ask what the software should accomplish, what could break, and what the user needs before choosing implementation details.

Make quality visible

Good work is not hidden in private effort. We document decisions, review interfaces, test behavior, and share progress clearly.

Care about the product

Engineering is better when the team understands customer workflows, business constraints, and the operational reality behind each feature.

Keep learning

AI, cloud, frontend, security, and product practices evolve quickly. We learn continuously and apply new ideas with judgment.

Hiring process

A straightforward process focused on communication, judgment, and craft.

  1. 01

    Introduce

    Send your background, work samples, interests, and the kinds of problems you want to solve.

  2. 02

    Conversation

    We discuss your experience, communication style, technical judgment, and what you want from your next role.

  3. 03

    Work review

    We review relevant projects or a practical exercise that reflects real ProgProspect work.

  4. 04

    Collaboration

    We evaluate how you reason through product constraints, implementation tradeoffs, and user experience.

  5. 05

    Offer

    If there is a strong fit, we define role expectations, working model, and growth path clearly.
