How engagements work

A practical delivery model for reducing uncertainty before code becomes expensive.

  1. 01

    Audit

    We review goals, users, workflows, systems, constraints, and current technical risk.

  2. 02

    Plan

    We define scope, architecture, milestones, quality gates, and release expectations.

  3. 03

    Build

    We execute with transparent progress, modular systems, and disciplined engineering standards.

  4. 04

    Validate

    We test usability, performance, accessibility, security, reliability, and business acceptance.

  5. 05

    Launch

    We support deployment, monitoring, documentation, handoff, and the next product iteration.
